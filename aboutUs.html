About Us

A better way to find available coaches.

Hello! Thank you for visiting Nomad Coach! We are thrilled to have you here and are excited to share with you our vision for creating this application. Nomad Coach was designed to help create a network for Gym owners as well as professional fitness trainers of all specialties connect fast and easily. They can filter their search results to find the best match for their gym needs! The Nomad rating system allows for a clear history of reviews to indicate past performance, so there is more transparency for both parties.
